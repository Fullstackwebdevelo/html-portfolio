About Me

I am an aspiring Full Stack Web Developer with a passion for technology, problem solving, and continuous learning.

My interest in web development comes from a love for tech and a curiosity about how digital products are built. I enjoy solving problems, learning new skills, and challenging myself to grow. As a patient and disciplined person, I found web development to be a career path that aligns perfectly with my strengths and interests.

My goal is to become a skilled Full Stack Developer who can freelance, build products, and create solutions that solve real-world problems. I am inspired by innovative technologies that make a meaningful impact, and I hope to build something valuable that helps people in the future.

As both a learner and a business owner, I understand the importance of discipline, dedication, and continuous improvement. These values guide me as I grow in both technology and entrepreneurship.

This portfolio documents my journey, projects, and progress as I work toward becoming a professional developer. I am excited to keep learning, building, and turning ideas into reality.
